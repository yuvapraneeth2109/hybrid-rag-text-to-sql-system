Table Name: DM_AA_ARRANGEMENT_ACTIVITY

Description:
Stores activity history performed on customer arrangements, including authorization, effective dates, transaction amounts, and product context.

Columns:
- ACTIVITY: Type or name of activity performed on the arrangement
- ARR_SEQUENCE: Sequence number ordering activities within an arrangement
- ARRANGEMENT: Identifier of the customer arrangement or contract
- AUTHORISER: Person or system that authorized the activity
- EFFECTIVE_DATE: Date when the activity takes effect
- ORIG_TXN_AMT: Original transaction amount associated with the activity
- CO_CODE: Company or branch code responsible for the activity
- BUSINESS_DATE: Banking business date for processing and reporting
- PRODUCT: Product or service associated with the activity

Primary Key:
(ARRANGEMENT, ARR_SEQUENCE)

Usage:
Used for arrangement activity timelines, audit tracking, authorization review, and transaction history analysis.
