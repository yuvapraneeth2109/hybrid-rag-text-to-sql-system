Table Name: DM_EB_QUERIES_ANSWERS

Description:
Stores electronic banking customer queries and answers including lifecycle status, customer linkage, and business date tracking.

Primary Key:
- EB_QA_SK (surrogate)

Business Key:
- EB_QUERIES_ANS_ID + BUSINESS_DATE

Key Columns:
- EB_QUERIES_ANS_ID: Query or answer identifier
- CUSTOMER_NO: Customer reference
- STATUS: Query status
- CREATION_DATE: Creation timestamp
- BUSINESS_DATE: Reporting business date
- CO_CODE: Company or branch code

Usage:
Used for customer support reporting, query lifecycle tracking, service quality analysis, and electronic banking operations monitoring.
