Table Name: DM_AA_PRODUCT

Description:
Stores product master information including availability dates, lifecycle status, and descriptive details.

Columns:
- PRODUCT (Primary Key): Unique identifier or name of the product
- PRODUCT_STATUS: Current status such as active or discontinued
- AVAILABLE_DATE: Date when the product becomes active
- CAT_EXPIRY_DATE: Expiry date of the product or product category
- DESCRIPTION: Detailed description of the product
- M: Generic numeric field for internal calculations
- S: Generic numeric field for sequencing or system metrics

Usage:
Used for product lifecycle tracking, product-based reporting, analytics, and regulatory classification.
