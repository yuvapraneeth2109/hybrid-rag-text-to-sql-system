Table Name: DM_CURRENCY

Description:
Stores currency master data and foreign exchange rates by business date.

Primary Key:
- CURRENCY_CODE + BUSINESS_DATE

Key Columns:
- CURRENCY_CODE: Currency identifier
- BUSINESS_DATE: FX rate effective date
- MID_REVAL_RATE: Mid-market exchange rate
- BUY_RATE: Buy exchange rate
- SELL_RATE: Sell exchange rate
- NO_OF_DECIMALS: Currency precision
- COUNTRY_CODE: Country associated with the currency

Usage:
Used for currency conversion, FX revaluation, balance reporting in local currency, and financial analytics.
