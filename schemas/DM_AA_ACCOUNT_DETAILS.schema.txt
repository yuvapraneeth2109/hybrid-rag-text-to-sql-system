Table Name: DM_AA_ACCOUNT_DETAILS

Description:
Stores account arrangement aging, dormancy, and maturity information used by banking operations teams.

Columns:
- ID (Primary Key): Unique identifier for the account aging record
- ARR_AGE_STATUS: Aging status of the arrangement such as current or overdue
- ARR_DORMANCY_STATUS: Indicates whether the arrangement is active or dormant
- ARRANGEMENT_ID: Identifier of the banking arrangement or contract
- MANUAL_AGEING: Indicates whether aging was applied manually or by system
- CREATED_DATE: Date when the aging record was created
- BUSINESS_DATE: Bank business date for which aging applies
- MATURITY_DATE: Maturity or end date of the arrangement

Usage:
Used to identify overdue, dormant, matured, or manually aged banking arrangements.
