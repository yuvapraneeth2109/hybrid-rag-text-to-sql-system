Table Name: DM_DATES

Description:
Calendar date dimension table providing week, month, quarter, and year attributes for reporting.

Primary Key:
- BUSINESS_DATE

Key Columns:
- BUSINESS_DATE: Calendar date
- CURRENT_WEEK: Week of year
- CURRENT_MONTH: Month number
- CURRENT_YEAR: Year
- CURRENT_QUARTER: Quarter
- MONTH_END_DATE: Month-end date

Usage:
Used for time-based aggregations, trend analysis, month-end reporting, and joining with fact tables by business date.
