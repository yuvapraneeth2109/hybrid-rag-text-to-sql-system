Table Name: DM_AA_ARR_ACCOUNT

Description:
Stores arrangement-level account classification, sector, loan purpose, and reporting data by business date.

Columns:
- ID (Primary Key): Unique identifier for each arrangement account record
- BUSINESS_DATE: Banking business date representing the arrangement snapshot
- ARRANGEMENT_ID: Identifier of the customer arrangement or contract
- PROPERTY: Code describing the arrangement type
- DATE_SEQ_NO: Sequence number for ordering or processing
- CSD_CLASS: Customer or credit class
- CSD_LOAN_PURPOS: Loan purpose
- CSD_SUB_CLASS: Sub-classification
- CSD_SUB_SECTOR: Sub-sector classification
- CO_CODE: Company or branch code
- AUTHORISER: Authorizing person or system
- CSD_MOB_BANK: Mobile or digital banking information
- CREATED_DATE: Record creation timestamp
- CSD_SECTOR: Sector classification
- CATEGORY: High-level reporting category

Usage:
Used for arrangement classification, sector analysis, loan purpose reporting, and business-date–based snapshots.
