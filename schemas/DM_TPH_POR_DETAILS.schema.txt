Table Name: DM_TPH_POR_DETAILS

Description:
Stores supplementary and extended information related to TPH payment orders.
Used to capture additional attributes, local fields, responses, and accounting references
that do not fit directly into the main payment order table.

Columns:
TPH_DETAIL_ID – Unique identifier for the payment order detail record
TPH_POR_ID – Reference to the parent TPH payment order
ADDITIONAL_INF_LINE – Free-text additional information or notes
LOC_FIELD_NAME – Name of a local or custom field
LOC_FIELD_VALUE – Value of the local or custom field
RESPONSE_DETAILS – System or external response details
ENTRY_IDS – Comma-separated list of related entry identifiers
ACCOUNT_NUMBER – Account number linked to the detail
BUSINESS_DATE – Business date of the detail record
M – Internal marker field
S – Internal marker field

Primary Key:
TPH_DETAIL_ID

Relationship:
Many detail records may exist for a single TPH payment order (TPH_POR_ID).

Usage:
Used for payment investigations, reconciliation, audit trails, extended messaging fields,
and operational analysis where additional payment attributes are required.
