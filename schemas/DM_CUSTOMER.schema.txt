Table Name: DM_CUSTOMER

Description:
Stores customer master data including identity details, KYC, risk classification, segmentation, and effective-dated history.

Primary Key:
- CUSTOMER_CODE + EFFECTIVE_START_DATE

Key Columns:
- CUSTOMER_CODE: Customer identifier
- CUSTOMER_TYPE: Individual or corporate
- CUSTOMER_STATUS: Operational status
- CALC_RISK_CLASS: System risk classification
- MANUAL_RISK_CLASS: Manual risk override
- COUNTRY: Customer country
- DATE_OF_BIRTH: Date of birth
- EFFECTIVE_START_DATE: Record effective start date
- EFFECTIVE_END_DATE: Record effective end date

Usage:
Used for customer profiling, KYC and AML compliance, risk assessment, segmentation analysis, and customer-level reporting.
