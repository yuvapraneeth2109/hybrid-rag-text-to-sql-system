Table Name: DM_ACCOUNT

Description:
Stores core account master data including customer linkage, contract reference, product, currency, limits, status, and effective dates.

Primary Key:
- ACCOUNT_SEQ_NO

Business Uniqueness:
- ACCOUNT_SEQ_NO + EFFECTIVE_START_DATE

Key Columns:
- ACCOUNT_SEQ_NO: Unique account identifier
- CUSTOMER: Customer linked to the account
- CONTRACT_CODE: Contract or arrangement reference
- PROD_CODE: Product code
- CURRENCY: Account currency
- STATUS: Account status code
- ACC_DEB_LIMIT: Debit limit
- EFFECTIVE_START_DATE: Account effective start date
- EFFECTIVE_END_DATE: Account effective end date

Usage:
Used for account-level reporting, customer exposure analysis, balance and limit checks, account lifecycle tracking, and operational queries.
