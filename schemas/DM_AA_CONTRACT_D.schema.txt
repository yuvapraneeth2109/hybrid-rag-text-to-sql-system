Table Name: DM_AA_CONTRACT_D

Description:
Stores contract-level details including product, customer, officer, financial terms, dates, and status by business date.

Columns:
- BUSINESS_DATE: Banking business date snapshot of the contract
- ARRANGEMENT_ID: Identifier of the customer arrangement
- CUSTOMER_CODE: Customer code linked to the contract
- LINKED_APPL_ID (Primary Key): Originating application identifier
- ACCOUNT_OFFICER_CODE: Account officer managing the contract
- BRANCH_CODE: Branch where the contract is maintained
- PRODUCT: Product associated with the contract
- PRODUCT_LINE: Product line classification
- CURRENCY_CODE: Contract currency
- LIMIT_REF: Credit or loan limit reference
- FIXED_RATE: Fixed interest rate
- MATURITY_DATE: Contract maturity date
- PAYMENT_START_DATE: Payment schedule start date
- PAYMENT_END_DATE: Payment schedule end date
- DEPT_OFFICER_NAME: Department officer handling the contract
- INSURANCE_NAME: Insurance associated with the contract
- MANUAL_CLASS: Manual classification
- SECTOR_NAME: Sector classification
- RESTRUCTURE_AMT: Restructured amount
- START_DATE: Contract start date
- PROFILE_ID: Customer profile identifier
- ARR_STATUS: Contract status
- HASH_COLUMN: Record hash for integrity
- ARRANGEMENT_SEQ_NO: Arrangement sequence number
- CO_CODE: Company code
- CUSTOMER: Customer identifier or name
- PRODUCT_GROUP: Product grouping
- ORIG_CONTRACT_DATE: Original contract creation date

Usage:
Used for contract reporting, product analysis, customer exposure, maturity tracking, and compliance reporting.
