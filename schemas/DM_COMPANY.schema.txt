Table Name: DM_COMPANY

Description:
Stores branch and company master information including location, zone, currency, and operational status.

Primary Key:
- BRANCH_CODE

Key Columns:
- BRANCH_CODE: Branch identifier
- BRANCH_NAME: Branch name
- STATUS: Operational status
- CO_CODE: Company code
- ZONE_CODE: Zone identifier
- ZONE_NAME: Zone name
- LOCAL_CURRENCY: Branch local currency
- CSD_CITY: Branch city

Usage:
Used for branch-wise reporting, zone analysis, company-level aggregation, operational dashboards, and regulatory reporting.
