Table Name: DM_AA_ACCOUNT_DETAILS_DETAILS

Description:
Stores detailed aging, billing, repayment, and schedule information for banking arrangements.

Columns:
- ID (Primary Key): Unique identifier for the account details record
- ARRANGEMENT_ID: Identifier of the banking arrangement or account
- AGE_ALL_DATE: Timestamp of the last aging calculation
- AGE_ALL_STATUS: Overall aging status such as current or overdue
- HOL_START_DATE: Start date of any holiday or payment deferral period
- M: Numeric field used for aging or calculation purposes
- NUM_PAYMENTS: Total number of payments made
- PAYMENT_DATE: Date of a specific payment
- REPAY_REFERENCE: Reference identifier for repayment transaction
- S: Numeric field used for scheduling or sequencing
- BILL_ID: Identifier for the bill
- SCHEDULE_TYPE: Type of repayment schedule such as EMI or Bullet
- BILL_TYPE: Type of bill generated
- BILL_STATUS: Current status of the bill
- SET_STATUS: Settlement or collection status
- BUSINESS_DATE: Bank business date for the record
- CREATED_DATE: Record creation timestamp
- AGING_STATUS: Current aging status of the account

Usage:
Used for payment tracking, billing status checks, aging recalculations, and repayment analysis.
