Table Name: DM_ACCOUNT_F

Description:
Stores account-level financial balances, limits, and status by business date for reporting and reconciliation.

Primary Key:
- ACCOUNT_SEQ_NO + BUSINESS_DATE

Key Columns:
- ACCOUNT_SEQ_NO: Account identifier
- BUSINESS_DATE: Balance snapshot date
- WORKING_BALANCE: Available working balance
- ONLINE_ACTUAL_BAL: Real-time balance
- LOCKED_AMOUNT: Blocked amount
- ACC_DEB_LIMIT: Debit limit
- STATUS: Financial record status

Usage:
Used for balance reporting, account reconciliation, customer exposure analysis, liquidity monitoring, and operational dashboards.
