Table Name: DM_PP_STATUSCODE

Description:
Reference table storing payment and transaction status codes including CSR and GPI classifications.

Primary Key:
- STATUSCODE

Key Columns:
- STATUSCODE: Payment or transaction status code
- STATUSDESCRIPTION: Status meaning
- CSRSTATUS: Customer service status
- GPISTATUS: Cross-border payment tracking status
- REASONCODE: Reason for the status
- CO_CODE: Company or branch association

Usage:
Used for payment lifecycle tracking, transaction monitoring, customer service workflows, and SWIFT/GPI reporting.
