Table Name: DM_ATM_TRANSACTION

Description:
Stores ATM and POS transaction records including amounts, accounts, charges, reversals, network details, and processing status.

Primary Key:
- ATM_TXN_SK (surrogate)

Business Key:
- ATM_TXN_ID + BUSINESS_DATE

Key Columns:
- ATM_TXN_ID: ATM transaction identifier
- DEBIT_ACCT_NO: Debit account
- DR_CUSTOMER_ID: Customer identifier
- TXN_AMOUNT: Transaction amount
- TRANS_STATUS: Transaction status
- TXN_TYPE: Type of transaction
- BAL_AFT_TXN: Balance after transaction
- BUSINESS_DATE: Reporting business date

Usage:
Used for ATM/POS transaction reporting, reconciliation, fraud analysis, customer activity analysis, and operational dashboards.
