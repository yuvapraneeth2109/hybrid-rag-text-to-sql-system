Table Name: DM_GL_BAL_CRB_F

Description:
Stores general ledger and credit balance facts by contract, line number, and business date.

Primary Key:
- CONTRACT_CODE + LINE_NO + BUSINESS_DATE

Key Columns:
- CONTRACT_CODE: Contract or account identifier
- LINE_NO: GL line identifier
- BUSINESS_DATE: Balance snapshot date
- LCY_BALANCE: Local currency opening balance
- LCY_CLOSING_BALANCE: Local currency closing balance
- USD_EQU_BALANCE: USD equivalent balance
- CURRENCY: Account currency
- SECTOR: Sector classification
- CUSTOMER_ID: Customer reference

Usage:
Used for GL reporting, regulatory submissions, credit exposure analysis, sector-wise reporting, and financial consolidation.
