Table Name: DM_STMT_ENTRY

Description:
Stores account statement transaction entries with amounts, references, balances, and business date tracking.

Primary Key:
- STMT_ENTRY_SK (surrogate)

Business Key:
- ID + BUSINESS_DATE

Key Columns:
- ACCOUNT_NUMBER: Account identifier
- CUSTOMER: Customer reference
- TRANSACTION_CODE: Transaction type
- AMOUNT_LCY: Transaction amount
- RUNNING_BALANCE_LCY: Balance after transaction
- VALUE_DATE: Effective transaction date
- BUSINESS_DATE: Reporting business date

Usage:
Used for account statements, transaction history, balance reconstruction, customer queries, and operational reporting.
